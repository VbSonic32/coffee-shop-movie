Our Story

More than coffee —
a ritual

Nestled in the heart of Conroe, Ember & Oak was born from a simple belief: a truly great cup of coffee can anchor your entire day. We source beans from the finest estates in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — then roast them in-house to let every origin speak for itself.

Our space was designed to slow you down. Reclaimed wood, warm amber light, and the gentle percussion of an espresso machine. Come as you are — stay as long as you like.

Direct Trade

We partner directly with farmers, ensuring fair pay and exceptional quality at the source.

In-House Roast

Every bean roasted on-site, weekly, to guarantee peak freshness in every cup.

Seasonal Menu

Our offerings evolve with the seasons, celebrating the best flavors each time of year brings.

Community First

A neighborhood gathering spot since 2019 — proud to be part of Conroe's fabric.
